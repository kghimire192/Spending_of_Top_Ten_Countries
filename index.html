Introduction

G20, Group of Twenty, is the international forum for global economic cooperation. G20 comprises of the most developed as well as the largest growing economies in the world. The 10 of the G20 members used in the analysis include Brazil, China, Germany, India, Japan, South Korea, Russia, Saudi Arabia, United Kingdom, and the United States.


Overview

The data from The World bank website was used for this analysis. The goal is to understand the spending pattern of the top 10 countries in education sector, healthcare sector and military sector over the years (2011 to 2015).
